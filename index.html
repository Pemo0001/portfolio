Caveman Studio


A team effort from Copenhagen School of Technology & Design. We designed a website for producer & musician Lucas Delacroix, who runs Caveman Studio.
We started by doing research and having a conversation with Lucas about his wants & needs. Afterwards we delved into the design sprint. In the end we had a final designsystem and a prototype created in Figma.
We used the framework Astro to code the website, and also implemented a database via Supabase. The project also included making photo/video footage for social media, which you can also find on the website.

Girltalk webfeature


Another team effort from Copenhagen School of Technology & Design.
GirlTalk is a Danish community for teen girls and their parents, where they can obtain information about all kinds of struggles girls go through in their teen years.
We had to work with an existing designsystem, meaning the colours and fonts had already been decided for us going in to the project.
We went through the sprint design proces coming up with userstories and sketches. Again we used the component-based framework Astro to code the website.
A part of the project was to create infographics and other graphic material, which is why all the graphics and illustrations on the website is created by the team.

Vicuras


Another team effort from Copenhagen School of Technology & Design.
This project focused on UX and UI design, helping an existing business to optimize their website for a better user experience. We worked with the company Vicuras who sell massage-solutions to companies.
The company had an overwhelming amount of information in their menu & sites, therefore we worked to create a more precise UI that helps the user get the information they need in less time.
The design and coding of the website is a bit lacking, since the main focus was to create the right content. The website is coded in plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
